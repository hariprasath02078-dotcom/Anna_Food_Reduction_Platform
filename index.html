SIH problem statement — Smart Management of Food Storage & Waste Reduction

Every day, edible food
goes to landfill while

Anna Setu ("bridge of food") is a live ledger that connects anyone with surplus food — a restaurant, a wedding hall, a hostel mess, a household — to the nearest NGO or volunteer who can collect it before it spoils.

Method

Three entries in the ledger

No cold storage, no new infrastructure — just faster information between people who already exist on both sides of the problem.

01 — LIST

Donor posts surplus

A restaurant, hostel mess, event hall or household logs what's left, how much, and by when it needs to be collected — takes under a minute.

02 — MATCH

Nearest NGO is alerted

The ledger ranks open listings by distance and time-to-spoil, so the closest verified NGO or volunteer sees the most urgent food first.

03 — CLAIM

Pickup is confirmed

One tap claims the listing, the donor gets a confirmation, and the ledger logs the rescue — meals saved, become a running public record.
